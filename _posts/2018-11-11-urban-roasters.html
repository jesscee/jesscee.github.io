Website design and development

I built a highly-customised e-commerce website for Urban Roasters. The website helps people easily purchase once-off coffee and coffee subscription orders online.

I also designed the theme and suite of user interface patterns for Urban Roasters, ensuring a consistent look and feel across their e-commerce platform.

Logo design

I designed a unique logo for Urban Roasters. The "UR" logo mark subtley hints at coffee beans with the applied shape, while also incorporating the initials of the business name (Urban Roasters).
